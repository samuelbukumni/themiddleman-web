Privacy Policy

Effective: May 2026  ·  NDPR & NDPA 2023 Compliant

This policy explains what data The Middleman collects, why, how we protect it, and your rights under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023.

1. Data We Collect

  • Registration: Name, email, WhatsApp number, role
  • Verification: NIN reference (hashed — raw NIN never stored)
  • Location: Approximate zone only. Precise GPS is processed in real-time but never stored permanently
  • Listings: Photos, descriptions, pricing you post publicly
  • Payments (Phase 3): Transaction records only — no card numbers ever stored
  • Usage: Anonymised page visits and search queries

2. Why We Collect It

  • Contract: To provide the marketplace service
  • Legitimate interest: Platform security and fraud prevention
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with NDPR/NDPA
  • Consent: Marketing communications (withdraw anytime)

3. How We Protect It

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS with TLS encryption
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt — we cannot see your password
  • Database protected by Row-Level Security (RLS) — users can only access their own data
  • NIN hashed before storage — raw NIN never retained
  • Payment data handled entirely by Paystack/Stripe — never touches our servers

4. Who We Share Data With

We do not sell your data. We share only with: Supabase (database), Vercel (hosting), Paystack/Stripe (payments, Phase 3), and Nigerian authorities when required by law.

5. Your Rights (NDPR/NDPA)

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Fix inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request your data be erased (Right to be Forgotten)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a readable format
  • Withdraw consent: Opt out of marketing at any time

Email privacy@themiddleman.com.ng to exercise any right. We respond within 30 days.

6. Retention

  • Active account data: Kept while your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Removed within 30 days of request
  • Transaction records (Phase 3): Kept 7 years per Nigerian financial law

7. Children

We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If discovered, it is deleted immediately.

8. Changes

Material changes notified by email 14 days in advance. Current version always available here.

9. Complaints

Contact: privacy@themiddleman.com.ng — or lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission at ndpc.gov.ng
